OUR MISSION
We are calling on institutions and individuals to join
us in empowering and supporting young Africans as
they embark on careers in technology, and help us
create a brighter and more prosperous future for
Africa through the power of technology.

WE BRING AFFORDABLE TECH
COURSES TO AFRICAN COMMUNITIES

Are you a young African looking to kickstart your tech career
and become part of a supportive and inclusive global
community of tech professionals, we invite you to join TechUp
Africa and take advantage of the education, training, and
resources we have to offer- at no cost to you..

Opportunities

To connect young Africans
with opportunities that can help them reach
their full potential as tech innovators,
entrepreneurs, and leaders.

Education

To provide access to education,
mentoring, and resources that help
young Africans build the skills and
knowledge they need to succeed in tech.

Join Community

To create a supportive and inclusive
community of tech professionals who
can help each other grow and thrive.
